Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

At Snazzy Paws Grooming, we are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose your personal information, guided by the Act’s 13 Information Privacy Principles (IPPs).

Information We Collect

As per IPP 1 (Purpose of Collection) and IPP 2 (Source of Information), we collect personal information directly from you to provide grooming services. This may include:

  • Your name and contact details
  • Your dog's name, breed, and age
  • Your booking preferences (date and time)

We collect this information fairly and directly from you, in accordance with IPP 4 (Manner of Collection).

Why We Collect Your Information

In line with IPP 1, we collect your information only to:

  • Manage and confirm grooming appointments
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Maintain service records for quality and safety

We are transparent about our collection practices, as required by IPP 3 (What to Tell You).

How Your Information Is Stored

Your data is stored securely in accordance with IPP 5 (Storage and Security). We take reasonable steps to protect it from loss, misuse, or unauthorized access.

Your Rights

Under IPP 6 (Access to Personal Information) and IPP 7 (Correction of Personal Information), you have the right to request access to and correction of your personal data at any time. Please contact us via our Contact page to make such requests.

Accuracy and Retention

Before using your information, we take steps to ensure it is accurate, up to date, and complete, as required by IPP 8. We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for our services, in line with IPP 9 (Limits on Retention).

Use and Disclosure

We use your personal information only for the purposes for which it was collected, in accordance with IPP 10 (Limits on Use). We do not disclose your information to third parties without your consent, unless required by law, as outlined in IPP 11 (Limits on Disclosure).

Overseas Disclosure

We do not disclose your personal information outside of New Zealand. If that ever changes, we will ensure compliance with IPP 12 (Disclosure Outside New Zealand).

Use of Unique Identifiers

We do not assign unique identifiers to individuals beyond what is necessary for booking management, consistent with IPP 13 (Unique Identifiers).
